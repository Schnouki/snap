Get started

Download snap

The latest version is 3.1.7, released on 2016/06/25 (Changelog).

Production version
around 3 kB gzipped 		Development version

Other download options

The latest development version is on GitHub.

npm: npm install @alexlur/snap

Initialize Snap

Note: Remember to include a polyfill for String#includes. ES6 Map/Set should also be included if you are targeting IE 10 or lower.

Include Snap and initialize it near the end of your pages:

1 2 3 4 5 
...
<script src="snap.min.js" data-no-instant></script>
<script data-no-instant>snap.init();</script>
</body>
</html>

Snap is now activated on your website. Read those articles to get a sufficient level of proficiency with snap:

  • How Snap works will give you the correct mental model to deal with its mechanism.
  • Preloading will show you the different preloading methods available.
  • Blacklisting because not all links should be preloaded.
  • Events and script re-evaluation will help you make snap play nice with the rest of your JavaScript.

Snap isn’t always “plug and play”, you may need to adapt your site. That’s why reading the above articles is mandatory.
